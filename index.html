About Me

My name is Oscar Andersson and I'm a positive, ambitious and loyal person with an interest for development, both personal and on the web. I've spent the substantial part of my professional career in the Sports Betting business working for a company that consults its costumers to pick the right bet of the day. My role at the company regularly increased with more and more responsibility for editing the daily picks, keep other journalists to their deadline and for the last two and a half years I've also written my picks. This job gave me a lot of experience working with the statistic, research, marketing and writing.
